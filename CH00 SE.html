Interactive Model BME 0.1: Statistics

Instructions: This activity calculates the sample mean, sample standard deviation, and standard error of learning gains for ten students in each of two hypothetical classes: Class A and Class B. How far each student's learning gain is from the class average is shown in the third column of each table. The sample standard deviation is the square root of the sum of these squared distances divided by n-1, where n is the sample size. The standard error is the sample standard deviation divided by the square root of n.
